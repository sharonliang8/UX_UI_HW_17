About Me

Hello! Thanks for taking that extra step in getting to know me more. I am Sharon, and I recently graduated from Western University with a Bachelor of Health Science. While my academic background focused on healthcare, my love for art and creativity never faded. Following graduation, I decided to give myself the opportunity to explore my passions more through UX design. Since, I have enrolled in a UX/UI bootcamp with the University of Toronto and currently hold a graphic design internship at the United Nations. In my free time, I enjoy being in nature, spending time with family, and visitng my friends' pets.

Everyday, I challenge myself to learn more and to grow as a designer. While I have an interest towards healthcare technology, I am more than open to exploring other fields and their intersection with design. I don’t want to stick to what I know, I want to know it all.
